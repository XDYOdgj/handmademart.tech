Vacplus Moisture Absorbers – Keep Your Space Dry & Safe

Experience the perfect solution to protect your valuable items from humidity, moisture, and mold. Vacplus moisture absorbers are designed to absorb excess moisture quickly and effectively, making them ideal for any enclosed space.

With a powerful 500 ml capacity, these dehumidifier bags are perfect for tackling high moisture areas. The spherical calcium chloride inside ensures a fast and efficient drying process, while the one-way moisture permeable paper enhances performance and durability.

Thanks to the visible water collection feature, you can easily track the effectiveness of these desiccant bags. This means you always know when it's time to replace them, keeping your environment consistently dry and fresh. The fragrance-free formula is safe for fabrics, electronics, and valuables, eliminating unwanted odors and protecting your belongings from damage.

Vacplus dehumidifiers are compact and lightweight, making it easy to hang them in tight spaces such as bathroom cabinets, laundry rooms, or even inside your refrigerator. Whether you're storing clothes in your closet, preserving items in your basement, or keeping your RV dry and comfortable, these moisture absorbers are your reliable choice.
