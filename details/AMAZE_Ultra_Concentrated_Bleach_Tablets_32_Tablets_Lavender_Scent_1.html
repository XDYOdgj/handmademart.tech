AMAZE Ultra Concentrated Bleach Tablets

A safe and eco-friendly alternative to traditional liquid bleach. These powerful bleach tablets are designed for all types of cleaning, from laundry to bathroom surfaces, without the mess or strong chemical smells.

Why Choose AMAZE Bleach Tablets?

  • No Splash Handling: Safe and easy to use, making it ideal for households with children or pets.
  • Less Waste: With 73% less packaging and 95% less weight than liquid bleach, these tablets are a more sustainable cleaning choice.
  • Multi-Purpose Cleaning: Effective for laundry, toilet, and hard, nonporous surfaces like tiles, showers, and countertops.
  • Dissolves in All Wash Cycles: Works in both standard and HE washing machines, including cold water, for consistent results.
  • Convenient Design: Compact and easy to store, these tablets offer a practical solution for everyday cleaning needs.

How to Use

For laundry, simply add one tablet for light loads and two for heavily soiled items. In your bathroom, dissolve a tablet in the toilet bowl and let it work before brushing and flushing. For multipurpose cleaning, drop one tablet into a gallon of water and use as needed. The easy-dissolve formula ensures powerful cleaning performance every time.

The AMAZE Difference

These eco-friendly cleaning tablets combine strength with convenience. They eliminate the need for harsh chemical smells, provide a long-lasting cleaning solution, and are kinder to the environment. Whether you're cleaning clothes, toilets, or bathroom surfaces, AMAZE delivers spotless, odor-free results with ease.
