Go Green with UNNI Compostable Kitchen Bags

These eco-friendly, compostable bags are designed to support sustainable living while making your kitchen waste management more efficient and reliable. With an impressive 13-gallon capacity, they're perfect for households looking to reduce their environmental footprint without compromising on convenience.

Why Choose UNNI?

Constructed with heavy-duty 0.9 mil thickness, these bags are built to last and can handle wet or heavy food scraps without tearing. They're also certified for both U.S. BPI and Europe OK compost HOME standards, ensuring they break down safely and effectively in compost environments.

Eco-Conscious Design

The tall design of these compostable food scrap bags fits most kitchen bins and compost containers, offering a snug and secure fit for daily use. Each roll contains 50 bags, so you'll always have a supply ready. By choosing UNNI, you're helping to reduce single-use plastics and contribute to a cleaner, greener planet.

Keep these bags in a cool, dry place for best results, and use them within the first year for optimal composting performance. Embrace the change and make your home more sustainable with UNNI compostable kitchen bags.
